What is the car depreciation?
Car depreciation is the difference between a car's value when it's bought and it's value when it's sold.
How to calculate car depreciation?
To calculate car depreciation you should use the following formula:
car depriciation = value of the car when it's purchased - value of car after a specific number of years
Why use car depreciation?
The car depreciation is sued in order to know how much does the value of the car has decreased over the years.
